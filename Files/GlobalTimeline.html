🌍 World Peace Timeline 🕊️

Major Peaceful Developments Since 1850

📜1864
First Geneva Convention
The foundation of international humanitarian law, establishing protection for wounded soldiers and medical personnel during warfare.
⚖️1899
First Hague Peace Conference
International peace conference establishing laws of war and creating the Permanent Court of Arbitration for peaceful dispute resolution.
🏛️1920
League of Nations Founded
First worldwide intergovernmental organization aimed at maintaining world peace and preventing wars through collective security.
🕊️1945
United Nations Established
Formation of the UN with the primary purpose of maintaining international peace and security, promoting international cooperation.
📋1948
Universal Declaration of Human Rights
Fundamental document establishing universal human rights as the foundation for freedom, justice, and peace in the world.
🤝1957
Treaty of Rome - European Integration
Beginning of European integration process, transforming war-torn Europe into a peaceful union of democratic nations.
☢️1963
Nuclear Test Ban Treaty
Partial Test Ban Treaty banning nuclear weapons tests in atmosphere, outer space, and underwater, reducing nuclear tensions.
🧱1989
Fall of Berlin Wall
Symbolic end of Cold War division, reuniting families and nations, marking a victory for peaceful democratic movements.
🏛️1998
International Criminal Court
Establishment of permanent tribunal to prosecute individuals for genocide, crimes against humanity, and war crimes.
🌱2015
Paris Climate Agreement
Global cooperation to combat climate change, representing unprecedented international collaboration for planetary peace.
